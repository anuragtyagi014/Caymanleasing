FAQ'S

What funds are required to get behind the wheel in the LEASE-TO-OWN program.

We require you to pay your first month, a refundable security deposit, and account setup. Pricing varies by vehicle so please see the Lease-to-Own section above.

What funds are required to get behind the wheel in the LEASE program.

We require you to pay your first month, a refundable security deposit, and account setup. Pricing varies by vehicle so please see the Lease section above. Minimum term is 6-months. Account setup is waived on 12-month terms. Clients can lock in pricing for up to 24-months.

Do I need to schedule a test drive or can I just drop-in?

Due to space constraints, we do not keep the majority of our vehicles at our office, so scheduling a test drive ensures the vehicle you want to see is here when you come in!

When is the Refundable Security Deposit refunded?

The refundable security deposit is refunded AFTER successful lease term completion in accordance with the lease agreement.

Where are you located?

We are located upstairs in the Cayman Falls plaza. (Across from the Westin Hotel above Eats and Legendz on West Bay Road).

I’m new to the island, can I qualify?

Yes – Welcome to Cayman!

Do I have to pay for car insurance?

  • LEASE-TO-OWN Program: Insurance is billed separately monthly.
  • LEASE Program: Insurance is already included in the monthly rate.

The question I have isn’t in this list. What should I do?

No worries, give us a call at 945-2211 and we are happy to answer any other questions you might have!

What is the minimuam age you will approve?

  • LEASE-TO-OWN Program: 22 years and older.
  • LEASE Program: 25 years and older.
